MTG List Comparator

Find trade matches by comparing any two Moxfield lists.

Paste any two public Moxfield URLs and this tool will find matching cards between them — perfect for finding trade opportunities.

Supported list types:
📋 Decks ⭐ Wishlists 📦 Collections 📒 Binders

Example: Paste your wishlist as List 1 and someone's binder as List 2 to instantly see which cards they have that you need.

Cards are matched in 5 priority levels — from exact match (same set, collector number, foil) down to same card name in any set.
